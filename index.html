The Story

What Is Classy Girls?

Classy Girls is a hand-drawn digital art collection created by Aliyah. Every character is carefully designed with her own personality, style, and story. More than a collection, Classy Girls is a growing world filled with creativity, friendship, confidence, and self-expression.

Unlike traditional collections generated purely by algorithms, every brushstroke, outfit choice, and hairstyle in Classy Girls is placed with artistic intention. It is an exploration of identity and sisterhood in the digital era.

Meet The Artist

Hi, I'm Aliyah.

I am a 24-year-old computer science graduate from Egypt with a deep, lifelong passion for digital illustration and traditional art.

Classy Girls began as a simple sketch in my digital sketchbook and evolved into a growing collection of unique digital characters. Every girl is hand-drawn with care, inspired by individuality, creativity, friendship, and beauty.

My goal is to create a world where every character feels alive and every artwork tells a story. Thank you for stepping into my sketchbook.

Classy Girls is a hand-drawn digital art collection created by Aliyah. Every character is carefully designed with her own personality, style, and story. More than a collection, Classy Girls is a growing world filled with creativity, friendship, confidence, and self-expression.

The collection will consist of 4,848 unique digital artworks on the Ethereum network. Each artwork represents an individual girl with her own dreams and styles.

All artworks are created by Aliyah, a digital illustrator and founder. Every piece starts as a hand-drawn concept before being digitized, colored, and completed with Aliyah's signature styling.
